About the filter

Our color filter was developed to assist with color blindness within the red/green spectrum. Utilizing the Python programming language we were able to design a filter that would identify various shades of red and green within a photo. Realizing many individuals experiencing color blindness are unable to view common workplace documents or differentiate everyday items our filter seeks to make this differentiation more simple.
